The California Aggie

The California Aggie is the UC Davis student run newspaper. In 2017, it was brought back into print publication for the first time in three years. I participated in the "Print the Aggie" campaign by brainstorming marketing strategies on increasing the presence of the newspaper on campus and within the Davis community. The campaign needed support from at least 20% of the entire student body population in order to pass.

From November 2016 until September 2017, I was a graphic designer that made graphics to go along with articles. Starting in September 2017, I switched over to being a layout artist that formatted and designed the layout of the newspaper.

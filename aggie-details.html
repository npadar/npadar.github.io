The California Aggie

The California Aggie is the UC Davis student run newspaper. It was recently put back into print publication for the first time in three years. I participated in the campaign by brainstorming marketing strategies on increasing awareness of the presence of the newspaper. The campaign needed support from at least 20% of the entire student body population in order to pass. From November 2016 until September 2017, I was a graphic designer that made graphics to go along with articles. Starting in September 2017, I switched over to being a layout artist that formatted and designed the layout of the newspaper.

Project: Design a Tumblr Theme

Design a custom Tumblr theme by using the existing Tumblr framework and variables. Incorporate loops into blog posts.

HTML5/CSS3/Tumblr Framework

Travel

A Tumblr blog about popular and exciting travel destinations around the world.

Mockups

I developed two different mockups for the custom Tumblr theme using Adobe Illustrator. I experimented with various column layouts to explore past the traditional one column Tumblr design while keeping a minimalistic feel to the blog.

Final

I ended up going with the design of my first mockup keeping in mind the minimalistic theme I wanted to portray. The custom theme works with personal blog posts as well as reblogs.

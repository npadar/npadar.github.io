Maze Nails Branding

Branding

The purpose of this project was to familiarize myself with the standards of branding. Proper application of an organization's visual assets such as their logo, is critical for building a strong brand awareness and maintaining the integrity of a brand. I created the branding guidelines for designers to use when creating any merchandise for Maze Nails. Marketing assets include a business stationery system, clothing, hats, boxes, signs, and transportation.

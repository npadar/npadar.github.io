UC Davis Undergraduate Education

UC Davis Undergraduate Education oversees eleven different departments that support students, staff, and faculty. My job as the graphic design assistant was to streamline the design process by meeting with clients from those departments and implementing their design needs. I have worked on numerous projects including booklets, flyers, postcards, posters, and brochures. Each project adheres to the UC Davis brand guideline.

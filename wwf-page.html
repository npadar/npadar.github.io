Project: Design a Non-Profit Website

Design a website with three webpages for an existing or made up non-profit.

HTML5/CSS3

World Wildlife Fund

Their mission is to conserve nature and reduce the most pressing threats to diversity of life on Earth.

Wireframes

After extensively researching exisitng non-profits to identify key components included on their websites, I began to brainstorm ideas for my three-page non-profit project. To build this non-profit website, I first started out with making wireframes using the program wireframe.cc This helped me organize the basic layout and placement of content for each page. I estimated the placement and dimensions of what I wanted the content to look like.

Mockups

I then developed three different mockups for the homepage using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. This gave me a rough idea on how the website would turn out and what elements I would want to include in my final project.

Final

I ended up going with the general design of my second mockup, transferring the overall look of the design into the About Us and Work webpage.
